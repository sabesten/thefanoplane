The Monograph

The Fano Plane in Physics is a research monograph that develops the mathematical framework connecting the Fano plane $\mathrm{PG}(2,7)$, the lattice $\mathbb{Z}_7^3$, and the exceptional Lie group $G_2$ to problems in discrete geometry, representation theory, and flavor physics.

The central object is the $G_2$-equivariant projection $S^7 \to \mathbb{Z}_7^3$, which maps the continuous 7-sphere onto a discrete lattice while preserving the action of the exceptional symmetry group. This projection leads to:

  • The impossibility theorem: no perfect order-7 magic cube exists over $\mathbb{Z}_7$ (637 constraints, 0 solutions among $3.3 \times 10^8$ invertible matrices)
  • A 294-element stabilizer subgroup with 14 conjugacy classes and a character table featuring 6 one-dimensional and 8 six-dimensional irreducible representations
  • Heuristic connections to the Cabibbo angle ($\sin(\pi/14) \approx \lambda$, 1.10% deviation from PDG) and the CKM matrix

Why Seven?

The number 7 is the smallest prime for which the Fano plane exists — a projective plane of order 2 with exactly 7 points and 7 lines. This combinatorial object encodes the multiplicative structure of the octonions and the automorphism group $G_2$. The journey from the ancient Luoshu (a $3 \times 3$ magic square over $\mathbb{F}_3$) to the impossibility of its 3D order-7 analogue traces a path through finite geometry, representation theory, and algebraic topology.

本书简介

《Fano平面与物理》是一部研究专著，建立了Fano平面 $\mathrm{PG}(2,7)$、格 $\mathbb{Z}_7^3$ 与例外李群 $G_2$ 之间的数学框架，并将其应用于离散几何、表示论和味物理问题。

核心对象是 $G_2$ 等变投影 $S^7 \to \mathbb{Z}_7^3$，它将连续的7-球面映射到离散格上，同时保持例外对称群的作用。该投影导致：

  • 不可能性定理：$\mathbb{Z}_7$ 上不存在完美的7阶幻立方（637个约束，$3.3 \times 10^8$ 个可逆矩阵中0个解）
  • 294元稳定子群，含14个共轭类，特征标表包含6个1维和8个6维不可约表示
  • 与Cabibbo角（$\sin(\pi/14) \approx \lambda$，与PDG偏差1.10%）和CKM矩阵的启发式联系

为什么是七？

7是Fano平面存在的最小质数——阶为2的射影平面恰有7个点和7条线。这一组合对象编码了八元数的乘法结构和自同构群 $G_2$。从古老的洛书（$\mathbb{F}_3$ 上的 $3 \times 3$ 幻方）到其3D 7阶类比的不可能性，这段旅程穿越了有限几何、表示论和代数拓扑。
