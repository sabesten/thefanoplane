The isomorphism $PSL_2(7) \cong PSL_3(2)$ is a cross-dimensional outer automorphism: it connects the 2-dimensional projective line over $\mathbb{F}_7$ to the 3-dimensional projective plane over $\mathbb{F}_2$. This part explores how this bridge enables $\mathbb{Z}_7$-parameterized CKM matrices and suggests a topological origin for CP violation.

Numerical highlights: $\sin(\pi/14) = 0.2225$ vs. PDG $\lambda = 0.2250$ (1.10%); $A = V_{cb}/\sin^2(\pi/14) = 0.828$ vs. PDG $A = 0.825 \pm 0.015$ (0.37%).

同构 $PSL_2(7) \cong PSL_3(2)$ 是一个跨维外自同构：它将 $\mathbb{F}_7$ 上的2维射影直线与 $\mathbb{F}_2$ 上的3维射影平面联系起来。本部分探索这座桥梁如何使 $\mathbb{Z}_7$ 参数化的CKM矩阵成为可能，并提出CP违反的拓扑起源。

数值亮点：$\sin(\pi/14) = 0.2225$ 对比 PDG $\lambda = 0.2250$（1.10%）；$A = V_{cb}/\sin^2(\pi/14) = 0.828$ 对比 PDG $A = 0.825 \pm 0.015$（0.37%）。
