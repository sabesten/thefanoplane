This chapter establishes the fundamental objects of study: the Fano plane $\mathrm{PG}(2,7)$ as the projective plane over $\mathbb{F}_7$, the lattice $\mathbb{Z}_7^3$ as a discrete pre-geometry, and the $G_2$-invariant projection matrices with their orthogonal basis.

本章确立研究对象的基本结构：$\mathbb{F}_7$ 上的射影平面 $\mathrm{PG}(2,7)$、作为离散预几何的格 $\mathbb{Z}_7^3$，以及 $G_2$ 不变投影矩阵及其正交基。

Theorem 1.1 / 定理 1.1

Let $\Phi_s = \{v \in \mathbb{Z}_7^3 : \|v\|^2 = s \pmod{7}\}$. Then the projection matrix $P_s = \frac{1}{|G|}\sum_{g \in G} g \Pi_s g^{-1}$ is $G_2$-equivariant, where $G = \mathrm{Stab}(\Phi_s)$.

设 $\Phi_s = \{v \in \mathbb{Z}_7^3 : \|v\|^2 = s \pmod{7}\}$。则投影矩阵 $P_s = \frac{1}{|G|}\sum_{g \in G} g \Pi_s g^{-1}$ 是 $G_2$ 等变的，其中 $G = \mathrm{Stab}(\Phi_s)$。

Definition 1.2 / 定义 1.2

The canonical basis for the projection onto the 6-dimensional $G_2$-invariant subspace is given by:

$$w_1 = \frac{1}{\sqrt{42}}(7, -1, -1, -1, -1, -1, -1), \quad w_2 = \frac{1}{\sqrt{42}}(6, -1, -1, -1, -1, -1, -1)$$

投影到6维 $G_2$ 不变子空间的典范基为：

$$w_1 = \frac{1}{\sqrt{42}}(7, -1, -1, -1, -1, -1, -1), \quad w_2 = \frac{1}{\sqrt{42}}(6, -1, -1, -1, -1, -1, -1)$$

The $G_2$-equivariant projection $S^7 \to \mathbb{Z}_7^3$ maps the continuous 7-sphere to the discrete lattice. This chapter constructs the 294-element subgroup and shows that the projection preserves the constraint structure essential for the impossibility theorem.

$G_2$ 等变投影 $S^7 \to \mathbb{Z}_7^3$ 将连续的7-球面映射到离散格上。本章构造294元子群，并证明该投影保持了不可能性定理所必需的约束结构。

HD = 28: the homology deficit of the $\mathbb{Z}_7^3$ lattice. Three layers of constraints — axial (147), face-diagonal (294), body-diagonal (196) — sum to 637. The heuristic argument for the 147 → 196 transition is presented with honest annotation of the proof gap.

HD = 28：$\mathbb{Z}_7^3$ 格的同调亏缺。三层约束——轴向（147）、面对角（294）、空间泛对角（196）——总计637。147 → 196 的过渡采用启发式论证，并诚实标注了证明缺口。

Remark 3.1 / 注记 3.1

The passage from 147 to 196 in the deficit count is established by heuristic argument. A complete proof requires either a full Bredon cohomology computation or a combinatorial-topological alternative.

亏缺计数中从147到196的过渡由启发式论证建立。完整证明需要完整的 Bredon 上同调计算或组合拓扑替代方案。

The full automorphism group of $\mathrm{PG}(2,7)$ has order $|PGL(3,7)| = 33{,}784{,}128$. The stabilizer of the norm-sphere $\Phi_s$ is a 294-element subgroup with 14 conjugacy classes. Its character table features 6 one-dimensional and 8 six-dimensional irreducible representations, satisfying $6 \times 1^2 + 8 \times 6^2 = 294$.

$\mathrm{PG}(2,7)$ 的完全自同构群阶为 $|PGL(3,7)| = 33{,}784{,}128$。范数球面 $\Phi_s$ 的稳定子群为294元子群，含14个共轭类。其特征标表包含6个1维和8个6维不可约表示，满足 $6 \times 1^2 + 8 \times 6^2 = 294$。

A perfect order-7 magic cube must satisfy 637 simultaneous constraints. Exhaustive enumeration of $3.3 \times 10^8$ invertible matrices over $\mathbb{Z}_7$ yields 9,216 semi-perfect equivalence classes and 138 partial-diagonal classes, but zero perfect classes. This is the impossibility theorem.

一个完美的7阶幻立方必须同时满足637个约束。对 $\mathbb{Z}_7$ 上 $3.3 \times 10^8$ 个可逆矩阵的穷尽枚举得到9,216个半完美等价类和138个部分对角类，但零个完美类。这就是不可能性定理。
