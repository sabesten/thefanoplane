Part I: The Fano Plane over $\mathbb{Z}_7$

Part I：$\mathbb{Z}_7$ 上的Fano平面

The Impossibility Theorem

A perfect order-7 magic cube does not exist over $\mathbb{Z}_7$. Among $|\mathrm{GL}(3,7)| = 33{,}784{,}128$ invertible matrices, exhaustive enumeration yields 9,216 semi-perfect equivalence classes and 138 partial-diagonal classes, but zero perfect classes.

$\mathbb{Z}_7$ 上不存在完美的7阶幻立方。在 $|\mathrm{GL}(3,7)| = 33{,}784{,}128$ 个可逆矩阵中，穷尽枚举得到9,216个半完美等价类和138个部分对角类，但零个完美类。

Projection Existence

There exists a $G_2$-equivariant orthogonal projection $S^7 \to \mathbb{Z}_7^3$ with a 6-dimensional invariant subspace $V_3 \subset \mathbb{R}^7$. The canonical basis vectors are $w_1 = (7,-1,-1,-1,-1,-1,-1)/\sqrt{42}$ and $w_2 = (6,-1,-1,-1,-1,-1,-1)/\sqrt{42}$.

存在 $G_2$ 等变正交投影 $S^7 \to \mathbb{Z}_7^3$，具有6维不变子空间 $V_3 \subset \mathbb{R}^7$。典范基向量为 $w_1 = (7,-1,-1,-1,-1,-1,-1)/\sqrt{42}$ 和 $w_2 = (6,-1,-1,-1,-1,-1,-1)/\sqrt{42}$。

Stabilizer Structure

The stabilizer $\mathrm{Stab}(\Phi_s) \leq \mathrm{AGL}(3,7)$ has order $|\mathrm{Stab}| = 592{,}704$, yielding a 294-element quotient with 14 conjugacy classes: $\mathrm{C}_1$ (identity) + 8 classes of size 6 (order 7) + 5 classes of size 49.

稳定子群 $\mathrm{Stab}(\Phi_s) \leq \mathrm{AGL}(3,7)$ 的阶为 $|\mathrm{Stab}| = 592{,}704$，给出294元商群，含14个共轭类：$\mathrm{C}_1$（恒等）+ 8个size=6的类（阶7）+ 5个size=49的类。

Character Table

The 294-element group has exactly 14 irreducible representations: 6 one-dimensional and 8 six-dimensional, satisfying $6 \times 1^2 + 8 \times 6^2 = 6 + 288 = 294$. ✓

294元群恰有14个不可约表示：6个1维和8个6维，满足 $6 \times 1^2 + 8 \times 6^2 = 6 + 288 = 294$。✓

Luoshu as $\mathbb{F}_3$-Linear Map

The Luoshu magic square is the affine-linear function $f(x,y) = 2x + y \pmod{3}$ on $\mathbb{F}_3^2$. The magic property (constant sum on every line) is a linear-algebraic fact over $\mathbb{F}_3$, not a numerical coincidence.

洛书幻方是 $\mathbb{F}_3^2$ 上的仿射线性函数 $f(x,y) = 2x + y \pmod{3}$。其幻性（每条线上和为常数）是 $\mathbb{F}_3$ 上的线性代数事实，而非数值巧合。

Homology Deficit HD = 28

The constraint count for a perfect order-7 magic cube is 637 = 147 (axial) + 294 (face-diagonal) + 196 (body-diagonal). The homology deficit $\mathrm{HD} = 28$ measures the gap between the axial and body-diagonal constraint layers. The 147 → 196 transition is established by heuristic argument; a complete proof requires a full Bredon cohomology computation.

完美7阶幻立方的约束计数为 637 = 147（轴向）+ 294（面对角）+ 196（空间泛对角）。同调亏缺 $\mathrm{HD} = 28$ 度量了轴向与空间泛对角约束层之间的差距。147 → 196 的过渡由启发式论证建立；完整证明需要完整的 Bredon 上同调计算。
